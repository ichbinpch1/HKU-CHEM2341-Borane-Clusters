Concepts

Short, high-signal explanations you can expand into full lessons later.

Wade’s rules (quick map)

  • closo: (n+1) skeletal electron pairs (SEP)
  • nido: (n+2) SEP
  • arachno: (n+3) SEP
  • Practice: relate vertices (n) → shape family → predicted formula tendencies.
electron counting polyhedra structure

3c–2e bonding (intuition)

  • Bridging hydrogens stabilize electron-deficient frameworks.
  • Think “shared pair across three centers” instead of a missing bond.
  • Use bonding motifs to predict where bridges appear in small boranes.
